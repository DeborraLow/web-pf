Pawow
~ for Ueno Zoo ~
Pawow
Entertainment / Octorber 2014
Roles:
・Presenter
・Decoration
・Java Coding
Exhibition:
・Digital Contents EXPO 2014
Children love zoo. Children like adventure. In Ueno Zoo in Tokyo, they started to open their zoo in the evening in summer, but the customers’ satisfaction especially kids are not satisfactory because it’s too dark to see the animals. They want to enjoy watching the animals and experience as if they are in a real adventure. This is not an ordinary experience for them, so they want to share this to their friends and parents. Pawow can guide the kids to the animals by means of the vibration coming from Pawow glove. Furthermore, it enables children to hear the sounds and noise produced by animals. The closer you are, the louder the sound is. We offered a new way to let people enjoy it kinesthetically and naturally with animals, as if they are in real forests.
