Zanryu
iOS application / March 2015
Roles:
・Producer
・X-code Coding
・Database Coding
・Screen Design
Production Period:
・3 Weeks
Students study harder. They want to stay late to carry on their work in their laboratory, and they do it every day. In our campus, most pf these students have a part-time job, and they often go to school after they work and stay until midnight or even on weekends. Whenever they study over night at the campus, they need to submit a paper in advance, and also they need to check whether it is available or not. I made an application which enables us to check whether or not we can stay over night easily. Now that only our laboratory students can avail this app, I will extend this to let all the students use it.
