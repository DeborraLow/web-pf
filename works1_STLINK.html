STLINK
iOS application / November 2014
Roles:
・Producer
・Presenter
・X-code Coding
・Screen Design
Prize:
・Category Award
in e-zuka Smartphone App Contest 2014
What if lessons are more fun and interesting in university? What if college students will enjoy listening to their professors’ lectures? We believe that there will be many people who can consider many things such as social problems deeply, sensitively and sincerely. We also believe that they will make our society better in the future. In order to improve the lectures in a university, we just surveyed what factors cause us to feel bored, and we found out that not only students but also professors can't share their opinions easily. That's why we just offered it to them.
